Shinchan

It follows the adventures of the five-year-old Shinnosuke «Shin-chan» Nohara and his parents Misae and Hiroshi, baby sister, Himawari, dog, Shiro and his neighbours, and best friends and is set in Kasukabe, Saitama Prefecture, Japan. Crayon Shin-chan first appeared in 1990 in a Japanese weekly magazine called Weekly Manga Action, which was published by Futabasha. Due to the death of author Yoshito Usui, the manga in its original form ended on September 11, 2009. A new manga began in the summer of 2010 by members of Usui's team, titled New Crayon Shin-chan .
